About Me

Hey! I am Aditya and I am currently in my second year pursuing Software Engineering at the University of Waterloo. I love to use my technology stack to solve problems faced by the community.

I have over 6 years of programming experience in languages like C, C++, Java, Ruby, Python and C#. Over the past year, I have gained significant web development experience. I have experience with NodeJS, AngularJS, Flask, .NET and Ruby on Rails.

Apart from programming, I love tutoring. In the summer of 2014, I have clocked more than 50 hours of tutoring experience in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics and Computer Science. This experience motivated me to keep learning to maintain the role of a tutor and gave me satisfaction by helping others.

I have a keen interest in history, particularly World War 2 era. I keep this interest alive by watching historical documentaries in my free time. I also have a passion for photography.
